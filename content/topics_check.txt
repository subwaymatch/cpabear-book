AUD - A system of quality management.

AUD - SOX whistle-blower protections are designed to protect employees who report fraud from retaliation, such as wrongful termination, demotion, or other adverse employment actions.
The compensatory damages typically awarded include:
- Reinstatement to the same or equivalent position with the same seniority (so the employee is put back where they were before retaliation).
- Back pay with interest to compensate for lost wages during the period of wrongful termination or demotion.
- Compensation for special damages if the employee suffered additional harm (like emotional distress or other losses).
